OnlAud collects names, phone numbers and email addresses only for the purpose of comunication with our clients and in cases were our clients open an account with us, for log in credentials.

Private information collected by OnlAud will not be shared with any third parties. We do not upload videos on our site and therefore urls of videos that candidates submit are only used by our judges to watch the video during audition. We do not share those links with any third party and neither do we download the videos for any use other than auditioning.
